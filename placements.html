How We Get You Hired

Our 6-Step
Placement Journey

We don't just hand you a certificate and wish you luck. Every graduate goes through a structured, supported pathway to employment.

01
Portfolio & Showreel Build
In your final term, dedicated sessions help you curate, refine, and package your best work into a professional showreel and online portfolio that studios actually review.
02
Resume & LinkedIn Prep
Our placement team helps you craft a studio-standard resume and an optimised LinkedIn profile — two tools that are actively used in the hiring process by every studio in our network.
03
Mock Interview Sessions
Simulated technical and HR interviews with real industry feedback — so you arrive at the actual drive confident, prepared, and knowing exactly what each studio looks for in a candidate.
04
Campus Placement Drives
Multiple hiring companies visit our campus each quarter. You get direct access to HR managers and studio leads — interviews scheduled, results shared — all in one dedicated event.
05
Offer Negotiation Support
Our placement cell assists you in understanding and negotiating your first offer — salary benchmarks, role clarity, and contract review guidance included as part of the service.
06
Post-Placement Mentorship
Our relationship doesn't end at your first job. Alumni get access to continued mentorship, upskilling workshops, and referrals for future career moves through the MAAC alumni network.
