How to Reach Us

MAAC Ghaziabad RDC is easily accessible from all parts of Ghaziabad and Delhi NCR by road, metro, and bus.

By Metro
Nearest station: Raj Bagh (Blue Line). 5-minute auto-rickshaw ride to RDC Rajnagar from the station exit.
By Bus
Multiple UP Roadways and city buses run to RDC Rajnagar from Anand Vihar ISBT, Kaushambi, and Vaishali.
By Car / Cab
From NH-9 Delhi–Meerut Expressway, take the Rajnagar Extension exit. RDC Rajnagar is 2 km from the exit. Parking available nearby.
Nearby Landmarks
Located near Domino's Pizza RDC and Rajnagar District Centre. Easily identifiable — ask any local for MAAC RDC.

Ask on WhatsApp
What courses are available at MAAC Ghaziabad RDC Rajnagar? Courses
MAAC Ghaziabad RDC offers 8 major course categories: Visual Effects (ADVFX Plus), 3D Animation (AD3DIGHP, D3D, DAFM), Game Design (DGWA), Graphic Design (APDMD), Video Editing, AR & VR Design, Broadcast & TV Production, and Digital Design. We also offer Short-Term Pro courses in Autodesk Maya, 3ds Max, and After Effects. Visit our Courses page for full details.
What is the eligibility for admission? Admission
Most long-term programmes require a minimum of 10+2 (HSC) pass from any stream. Graduate students are also welcome. For Short-Term Pro courses, there is no formal qualification requirement — a basic interest in the subject is sufficient. We don't require prior creative experience; our curriculum is designed to take complete beginners to professional level.
How long are the courses and what are the timings? Duration
Long-term programmes range from 12 to 24 months depending on the course. Short-Term Pro courses are 3–4 months. We offer morning, afternoon, and evening batches (Mon–Sat) to accommodate students and working professionals. Specific batch timings are shared during the counselling session. New batches start monthly.
Are there any scholarships or EMI options available? Fees
Yes. MAAC Ghaziabad RDC offers merit-based scholarships for qualifying students, as well as EMI payment plans (0% interest available on select plans). Fee structure and scholarship eligibility details are discussed during your free counselling session. Call us at +91 784 000 2724 to learn more.
What is the placement assistance provided? Placements
We provide 100% placement assistance to all graduates. This includes portfolio review, resume building, mock interviews, and access to our campus placement drives where 50+ studios recruit directly. Our placement cell actively tracks your progress and connects you with opportunities even after graduation. Over 1,000 alumni have been placed across India and abroad.
Can I attend a demo class before enrolling? Demo Class
Absolutely — and we strongly encourage it. Every prospective student is invited to attend a free demo class in any programme of their choice. It's a chance to see the labs, meet the faculty, understand the curriculum, and get a feel for life at MAAC before making any commitment. To book your demo, fill in the form above or call us directly.
Is MAAC Ghaziabad RDC Rajnagar NSDC and MESC certified? Certification
Yes. MAAC Ghaziabad RDC Rajnagar is formally affiliated with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and certified by the Media & Entertainment Skills Council (MESC). Our major programmes also qualify for Autodesk Certified User credentials. These affiliations ensure that the certificates our students earn are recognised and valued by employers across the industry.
What software and hardware do students have access to? Infrastructure
Students have access to 60+ high-performance workstations (RTX 4080, 64 GB RAM, 4K displays), a Wacom Cintiq 24" drawing studio, copies of Autodesk Maya, 3ds Max, ZBrush, Nuke, Houdini, After Effects, Premiere Pro, Unreal Engine 5, Unity, Figma, Photoshop, and more. Our broadcast suite includes green-screen recording, capture stations, and DaVinci Resolve colour grading stations.
