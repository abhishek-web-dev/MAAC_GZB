Our Story

Welcome to
MAAC Ghaziabad RDC

Maya Academy of Advanced Creativity (MAAC) is India's premier institute for Animation, VFX, Filmmaking, and Game Design. The Ghaziabad RDC centre has been shaping creative professionals for over two decades — combining industry-standard curriculum with mentorship from seasoned professionals who've worked on Bollywood blockbusters and international productions.

Affiliated with NSDC and MESC, our courses are designed to make you placement-ready from day one — with hands-on studio projects, live briefs, and a network of 50+ hiring partners.

  • NSDC & MESC CertifiedGovernment-recognised credentials that carry weight with every employer.
  • State-of-the-Art LabsLatest Wacom tablets, Render Farms, and licensed industry software on every workstation.
  • Industry-Veteran FacultyLearn from professionals with credits on Bollywood & Hollywood projects.
  • 100% Placement AssistanceDedicated placement cell, portfolio review, and interview preparation support.
