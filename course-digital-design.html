Core Tracks

Different paths, same digital-design backbone.

This structure keeps the MAAC digital-design identity but frames it more clearly around broad creative outputs and employable portfolio pieces.

01

APDMC

Advanced Program in Digital Media Creation for learners who want a broad, modern skillset across visual communication and digital production.

  • Graphic design, content creation and media-led storytelling
  • Web, UI and presentation-friendly design skills
  • Branding and portfolio work across multiple formats
02

APDMD

Advanced Program in Digital Media and Design for students who want polished visual language across layouts, online media and screen communication.

  • Web design, visual hierarchy and digital brand systems
  • 2D communication ideas and publishing-aware layouts
  • Creative outputs suited to ads, campaigns and digital presence
03

DGWA

Programs in Graphics, Web and 2D Animation for students who want a simpler but strong route into design plus basic motion communication.

  • Graphics, web design and visual storytelling basics
  • Entry-level 2D animation support for digital content
  • Projects that can grow into a focused design portfolio
