The MAAC Legacy

Two Decades of
Shaping Creative Minds

MAAC — Maya Academy of Advanced Creativity was founded with a single, unwavering belief: that every creative mind in India deserves access to world-class training in the visual arts. Since opening its doors, the Ghaziabad RDC centre has been the launch-pad for thousands of animators, VFX artists, game designers, and graphic media professionals who now work at the top studios across India and beyond.

What started as a small training centre has grown into a full-spectrum creative media academy — equipped with render farms, Wacom studios, broadcast-grade editing suites, and faculty who have genuine credits on Bollywood blockbusters and international productions. Our curriculum is co-developed with the industry and continuously updated to reflect exactly what studios are hiring for today.

"Don't just animate — innovate. Our goal is not to produce graduates with certificates, but artists with voices." — MAAC Ghaziabad RDC, Centre Philosophy

Affiliated with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and the Media & Entertainment Skills Council (MESC), every programme at MAAC Ghaziabad RDC carries government-recognised certification that employers across the creative industry trust. Our dedicated placement cell has facilitated over 1,000 placements across 50+ hiring partners — from Yash Raj Films and Red Chillies Entertainment to Tata Elxsi and Technicolor.

Our DNA

Mission, Vision
& Core Values

M
Our Mission
To deliver world-class, industry-integrated creative media education that empowers every student — regardless of background — to build a meaningful, sustainable career in Animation, VFX, Game Design, and Digital Media. We achieve this through hands-on learning, mentorship from active industry professionals, and a relentless commitment to staying ahead of the curve.
V
Our Vision
To be recognised as the most trusted launchpad for creative professionals in North India — producing graduates who don't just fill roles, but define them. We envision a future where every alumnus of MAAC Ghaziabad RDC carries the confidence, the portfolio, and the network to thrive anywhere in the global creative industry.
01
Innovation First
We teach cutting-edge tools and workflows, not yesterday's software. Our labs are always running the latest industry-standard releases.
02
