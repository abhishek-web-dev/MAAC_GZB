Editing Workflow

From raw footage to final delivery.

The course flow mirrors real post-production steps, helping students understand not only software, but the full editing decision process.

Step 01

Ingest and organize

Import footage, sort takes, label media, sync audio, understand continuity and prepare the edit timeline.

Step 02

Assemble and rough cut

Arrange key shots, shape the story, control scene flow and build the first complete edit.

Step 03

Fine cut and picture lock

Refine pacing, remove weak frames, balance transitions, polish timing and lock the visual sequence.

Step 04

Finish and export

Add sound polish, titles, motion graphics, color grading, VFX cleanup and export for the required platform.
