XR Pipeline

From idea to immersive prototype.

The learning path follows a practical immersive workflow: plan, model, texture, light, integrate and present.

Stage 01

Concept and experience map

Define the AR/VR use case, audience, interaction goal, visual mood and spatial layout.

Stage 02

Create 3D assets

Model, texture and optimize props, environments, backgrounds and visual elements for real-time use.

Stage 03

Light and integrate

Build the scene, place assets, create mood, test scale and prepare Unity-style presentation.

Stage 04

Prototype and present

Turn the concept into an interactive demo, product visualization or immersive portfolio piece.
