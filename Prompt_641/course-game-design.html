Game Pipeline

From concept sketch to interactive game world.

The learning path mirrors how game art is built: ideate, model, texture, light, rig, assemble and present.

Stage 01

Concept and research

Build moodboards, understand gameplay context, define character, creature, prop and world direction.

Stage 02

Model and sculpt

Create 3D forms for props, environments, characters and creatures with strong shape language and scale.

Stage 03

Texture, rig and light

Add surface detail, material behavior, believable lighting, simple rigs and game-ready presentation polish.

Stage 04

Integrate and present

Prepare assets for Unity-style workflows, level presentation, interactive review and portfolio showcases.
