Production Workflow

From newsroom idea to finished broadcast package.

A strong broadcast creator understands the whole chain: what to say, how to edit it, how to design for screen, and how to polish the final sound and graphics for delivery.

Book Counselling
Stage 01

Script and Plan

Start with scriptwriting, story angle, visual references and a clear communication goal for the package.

Stage 02

Collect Assets

Organize footage, photos, audio, brand graphics and references so the edit can move fast and stay clean.

Stage 03

Edit and Design

Cut the story, build lower thirds, add motion graphics, shape 3D or digital design elements and maintain visual rhythm.

Stage 04

Mix and Deliver

Clean dialogue, balance music and effects, export broadcast-ready versions and present the work professionally.
